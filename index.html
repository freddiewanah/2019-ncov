About

We are a group of researchers from Rochester Institute of Technology(RIT) and Monash University.

We start this survey to understand how people seek information about the novel coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic and the difficulties that people encounter during the process. And see if the team can design tools and methods to help people better cope with this and future pandemics.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this study, you should feel free to ask them now or anytime throughout the study by contacting the principal investigator at RIT, Dr. Mingming Fan, Assistant Professor, School of Information, email: mingming.fan@rit.edu.
