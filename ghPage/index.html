Get Informed of Covid 19

The coronavirus pandemic is having a major impact on our usual daily activities. Millions of people around the world have been infected, resulting in thousands of deaths. People are constantly seeking information to understand the current COVID-19 situation. How do you seek this information? We want to hear from you.

Study

What does this study involve:

  • We want to understand how people are seeking information about COVID-19.
  • We try to understand if people encounter contradictory information and how to deal with such information inconsistency.
  • We want to know how people emotionally changed towards COVID-19.

About Us

We are a group of researchers from Rochester Institute of Technology(RIT) and Monash University.

By understanding people’s information seeking behaviour, the researchers hope to enable the government to better share their information in different ways, and also develop some methods to automatically detect contradictory information.
